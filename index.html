Our Little World

🌊

Sometimes my favorite place isn't a city… it's a memory with you. ❤️

I still smile thinking about our first trip to Daman 😍 — all that secret planning, all the drama, and the way we made everyone believe something else just to steal that one perfect trip together. It felt like our little adventure against the world.

And that romantic walk on the beach 🌊✨… the sound of the waves, the breeze in your hair, and the way you quietly held my arm 🥹🤍 I don't think you even realized what that did to me. In that moment, everything felt right. The way you held me… it melted my heart completely.

That trip wasn't just a getaway — it was the moment I knew how special "us" really is. 💕 Forever grateful for that memory… and for you. 💋✨

🤍

There's another memory that lives rent-free in my heart 🤍

I still remember when we had just come together… and life decided to send me to Delhi for work. Being away from you wasn't easy at all. Every single month, I would count days just to see you again.

I'd make excuses at the office, act all innocent, just so I could steal a few days to come see you 😅❤️ And no matter how hectic my schedule was, no matter how exhausted I felt… the moment you hugged me, everything disappeared.

That one hug of yours 🤗✨ — it fixed the distance, the stress, the long days… everything.

Those monthly visits weren't tiring. They were the highlight of my life. Because at the end of every long wait, there was you. 💕💋

🇨🇦

Some memories are beautiful… and a little painful at the same time 🤍

I still remember the day you got your visa. I was genuinely so, so happy for you — proud of you, excited for you, cheering for your new journey to Canada 🇨🇦✨ But somewhere deep inside, my heart felt heavier than ever… because I knew I wouldn't get to see you every month anymore.

You were glowing with excitement, ready to chase your dreams, and I never wanted to be a barrier in your path. Loving you means wanting the world for you — even if it means missing you more than I can explain. ♥️

That last see-off at the airport… it was one of the hardest moments for me. Holding back my tears, my emotions, my thoughts of not knowing when I'd see you again — it broke something inside me. The hug before you left felt too short and too long at the same time.

Till today, I'm holding onto that memory… and onto hope. Hoping to see you again soon. To hug you again. To stand next to you without a goodbye waiting at the end.

I just wish this distance drifts away soon… because my heart feels most complete when it's with you. 💕
